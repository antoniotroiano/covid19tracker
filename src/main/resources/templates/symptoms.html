COVID-19 symptoms and prevention
World
Most common symptoms
Fever
Dry cough
Fatigue
Severe symptoms
Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
Pain or feeling of pressure in the chest area
Loss of ability to speak or move
Less common symptoms
Body aches
Sore throat
Diarrhea
Conjunctivitis
A headache
Loss of taste or smell
Discoloration on fingers or toes or rash
Prevention
Stay at home
Wash your hands frequently for 20 seconds with soap
Social distancing
Wear a mask
Contact a doctor if you have fever, cough, or difficulty breathing
When you feel sick stay at home
More information at World health organization
